# Personas

## Primary User: DeepGem Project Manager

**Who:** The PM responsible for client engagements at DeepGem Interactive.

**Day-to-day:**
- Takes discovery calls and client meetings
- Translates conversations into scoped engagements
- Creates SOWs, change orders, and project documentation
- Manages client communication and expectations
- Uses ClickUp for project tracking

**Tools:**
- ClickUp (project management)
- PandaDoc (document signing)
- Meeting transcription tools (Fathom, Otter.ai, etc.)
- Claude Code / Cursor (with these skills)

**Pain points:**
- Time spent formatting documents that follow predictable patterns
- Risk of missing details from meeting notes
- Maintaining consistency across SOWs
- Translating messy conversation into structured scope

**What she needs:**
- AI that knows DeepGem's methodology and templates
- Asks smart clarifying questions instead of guessing
- Generates review-ready documents, not rough drafts
- Maintains the collaborative, warm tone that represents DeepGem

**Success for her:**
- "I paste meeting notes, answer a few questions, and get an SOW I can send with minor tweaks"
- "The AI catches things I might have missed from the conversation"
- "Documents are consistent and professional every time"

## Secondary User: Ziah (Founder/CEO)

**Who:** Reviews and signs SOWs, sets pricing strategy, takes some client calls directly.

**Needs from this system:**
- Confidence that SOWs follow the methodology
- Consistency in how DeepGem presents to clients
- Ability to quickly review PM-generated SOWs
- Templates that reflect his communication style

**Decision rights:**
- Pricing strategy and rates
- Template structure and tone
- Which capabilities to add to the skill set
